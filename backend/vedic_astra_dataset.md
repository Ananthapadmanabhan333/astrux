# VEDIC ASTROLOGY (JYOTISH) CORE DATASET
This is the foundational dataset for Vedic Astrological analysis.

## 1. THE 12 HOUSES (BHAVAS)
- 1st House (Lagna): Self, physical body, appearance, vitality, life path, early childhood.
- 2nd House: Wealth, accumulated finances, family lineage, speech, face, right eye.
- 3rd House: Courage, younger siblings, short journeys, communication, writing, efforts.
- 4th House: Mother, home, inner peace, properties, vehicles, general happiness.
- 5th House: Intellect, children, romance, past birth karmas (Purva Punya), speculation.
- 6th House: Enemies, debts, diseases, daily routines, litigations, service.
- 7th House: Marriage, business partnerships, legal bonds, spouse, public image.
- 8th House: Longevity, sudden events, inheritance, occult, research, chronic illness.
- 9th House: Dharma, father, gurus, higher education, long travel, luck, religion.
- 10th House: Karma, career, profession, fame, government, public standing.
- 11th House: Gains (Labha), elder siblings, networks, fulfillment of desires.
- 12th House: Losses, foreign lands, spirituality, moksha, isolation, bed pleasures.

## 2. THE 9 PLANETS (GRAHAS)
- Sun (Surya): Soul, father, ego, authority, government, vitality. (Rules Leo; Exalted Aries; Debilitated Libra).
- Moon (Chandra): Mind, emotions, mother, nourishment, public. (Rules Cancer; Exalted Taurus; Debilitated Scorpio).
- Mars (Mangal): Energy, courage, siblings, real estate, aggression, logic. (Rules Aries/Scorpio; Exalted Capricorn; Debilitated Cancer).
- Mercury (Budha): Intellect, business, speech, writing, nervous system. (Rules Gemini/Virgo; Exalted Virgo; Debilitated Pisces).
- Jupiter (Guru): Wisdom, expansion, children, wealth, husband (in female chart), religion. (Rules Sagittarius/Pisces; Exalted Cancer; Debilitated Capricorn).
- Venus (Shukra): Marriage, beauty, luxury, vehicles, arts, wife (in male chart). (Rules Taurus/Libra; Exalted Pisces; Debilitated Virgo).
- Saturn (Shani): Karma, delays, hard work, discipline, servants, chronic disease. (Rules Capricorn/Aquarius; Exalted Libra; Debilitated Aries).
- Rahu (North Node): Obsession, material desire, foreign things, illusions, sudden success, taboo.
- Ketu (South Node): Detachment, spirituality, moksha, isolation, sudden losses, genetics.

## 3. REMEDIES (UPAYAS)
- Sun: Offer water to Sun, recite Aditya Hridaya Stotram, wear Ruby (with advice).
- Moon: Donate milk/rice on Mondays, respect mother, wear Pearl.
- Mars: Recite Hanuman Chalisa, donate red lentils on Tuesday, wear Red Coral.
- Mercury: Feed green grass to cows, worship Vishnu, wear Emerald.
- Jupiter: Respect gurus, chant Om Gurave Namah, wear Yellow Sapphire.
- Venus: Respect women, donate white clothes on Friday, wear Diamond/Opal.
- Saturn: Feed crows/dogs, light mustard oil lamp on Saturday, worship Shiva, wear Blue Sapphire.
- Rahu: Donate coconuts/blankets, feed ants, worship Bhairava or Durga.
- Ketu: Feed street dogs, worship Ganesha, donate multi-colored blankets.

## 4. SADE SATI
- The 7.5 year transit of Saturn over the natal Moon sign, the sign before it, and the sign after it. 
- It is a period of intense karmic testing, hard work, delays, and structural life changes. It requires immense patience and discipline.

## 5. DASHAS (PLANETARY PERIODS)
Results during a Dasha depend strictly on the planet's dignity, house placement, and lordships. Malefics (Saturn, Mars, Rahu, Ketu) often bring struggle unless well placed. Benefics (Jupiter, Venus, unblemished Moon, Mercury) bring ease unless functionally malefic.
